PURJ Submissions

Attention!

The Spring 2014 submissions period has ended. Check back soon for the Fall submissions deadline

We accept submissions from all academic disciplines, and we are excited to announce that we are now accepting submissions for our very first edition of the journal!

To submit your research, follow these steps:

  1. Read the Submission Guidelines and ensure that your research paper follows the style and formatting rules
  2. Fill out the PURJ Submission Form. Be sure to read over the form carefully and complete it accurately
  3. Send your completed PURJ Submission Form along with a copy of your research paper in the appropriate file format (.doc, .docx, .rtf) to PURJSubmissions@gmail.com.

Please feel free to email PURJSubmissions@gmail.com with any questions about the submissions process.
